About Olailemi

Olailemi Adio Abdulrahaman (Data Science)

Hello! I'm a seasoned Data Analyst and Data Scientist with over a decade of experience. My journey began with a strong foundation in biological sciences and extended into a thriving career in the oil and gas sector, where I honed my managerial skills. My passion for data analysis led me to earn a Master's in Full Stack Data Science from 10Alytics in the UK.

Throughout my career, I have developed and refined my expertise in using powerful tools such as Excel, PowerBI, Tableau, SQL and Python. My experience spans across multiple industries, including oil and gas, e-commerce, and more. I have successfully completed internships and freelance work with renowned organizations like Mentorness and 10Alytics, where I specialized in machine learning and data analytics.

Currently, I work remotely as a Data Analyst for Visual Impact Multi-Biz Concept and Romona, providing valuable insights that drive business decisions. I also enjoy collaborating on platforms like Upwork, where I offer my analytical skills to a diverse range of clients.

One of my key projects includes developing a sentiment analysis model for Amazon e-commerce reviews, where I utilized techniques such as data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and training models like Logistic Regression, SVM, and Naive Bayes. My best-performing model, an SVM, was successfully deployed both locally and to the cloud using FastAPI and Streamlit.

Vision

My aim is to translate complex data into actionable insights, making data-driven decisions accessible to both technical and non-technical audiences. I'm dedicated to continuous learning and am always on the lookout for innovative ways to solve problems using data.

Welcome to my portfolio. Feel free to explore my projects and reach out if you would like to collaborate or learn more about my work!
